Windows 7

This course introduces users to the Windows 7 operating system, including the Desktop, the Taskbar, the Start Menu, and Windows Explorer.
 
Mac OS X

This course introduces users to the Mac OS X operating system, including the Desktop, the Dock, Finder, and the Status Bar. It also covers the major differences between Mac OS X and Windows 7.

 
Broadband 101

This course introduces users to the Internet and the World Wide Web,
as well as why they are important. Users will also learn about
Broadband and why it is crucial to life in the 21st century.
 
eSecurity


This class shows students how to keep themselves safe online. We show students how to create strong passwords, avoid online scams and phishing scams, how to avoid infecting their computers with viruses, and how to know whether or not the sites they visit are legitimate and secure.


 
eLearning


This course introduces users to what e-learning is and the different types of e-learning. It also covers the major e-learning websites and resources available. As well as the technology required for e-learning.

 
eGovernment


This course introduces users to e-government, as well as the goals, benefits, and challenges of e-government. It will also cover technologies used in e-government and e-government services available to users in Alabama.
 
eHealth


This course explains e-health and introduces users to what the goals of e-health are, as well as the benefits and challenges of e-health. Users will also be introduced to Consumer Health Services and the different parts of e-health.

 
eCommerce


This course introduces users to the basics of e-commerce. It also covers how to expand an existing business through e-commerce.

 

 
eHome


This course explains to users how home broadband access can improve their lives.
